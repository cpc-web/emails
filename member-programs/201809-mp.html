Summer is unofficially over, but it is still the perfect time to enjoy a picnic, toss a Frisbee, or stroll through the Park. As millions of students go back to school, at the Conservancy, we too are learning… about you! We’ve created a brief member survey to hear from you about why you support the Conservancy and what you like best about your membership. See below for more information and to take the survey.

This is also the perfect time for you to take advantage of special Central Park Perks that are only available to Central Park Conservancy members, such as reduced ticket prices for Official Central Park tours, discounted offers at select in-Park vendors, and exclusive members-only events, a few of which have been highlighted below. Thank you for your support of the Park, and we hope to see you here soon!

Woodland Walks

North Woods
Sunday, September 23 | 1:30 pm – 3:00 pm
FREE for all members | Register

Join our guides for this exclusive tour of the North Woods, to get a taste of the Adirondack Mountains in the middle of New York City. The North Woods is a favorite spot of birdwatchers and hikers, and an oasis of nature for wildlife.

Hound Hikes

Sunday, September 30 | 11:30 am – 1:00 pm
Sunday, November 11 | 11:30 am – 1:00 pm
FREE for all members | Register

Take one of our signature tours with your favorite furry family member and let our Central Park Conservancy guide give you an insider's look at some of the most iconic features of the world's greatest urban park. Enjoy a great variety of the scenic, sculptural, and architectural elements the Park has to offer.

Drawing Classes

Saturday, October 13 | 10:30 am – 12:00 pm
Saturday, November 17 | 10:30 am – 12:00 pm
FREE for all members | Register

Attendees will learn how to capture the beauty of the Park on paper in this drawing class, led by Centennial Trees of Central Park artist, Jessica Daryl Winer. No drawing experience is necessary.

Autumn Amble: A Tour of Central Park’s Foliage and Northern Forts

Monday, October 22 | 8:00 am – 9:30 am
FREE for Protector members ($250 and above)
Invitations will be mailed at the end of September

This tour will highlight the historic monuments and trees in the northeastern section of the Park. Coffee and pastries will be served prior to the tour.

Member Survey

We are interested in hearing your thoughts about the Conservancy and why you choose to be a member. Take a few minutes to fill out a brief member survey. As a thank you, you will be entered to win an invitation for two to attend the Conservancy’s annual New York City Marathon Breakfast and Viewing Party on Sunday, November 4. Take the survey.

To access your Central Park Perks discounts, please use your valid membership card with discount code DENE10. If you have questions about your membership, please contact our membership office at membership@centralparknyc.org or 212.310.6672. Thank you!

The Central Park Conservancy raises 75% of Central Park's annual budget and is responsible for the day-to-day maintenance and operations that keep the Park beautiful.
